Fear Of Loud Sounds

Animation

Project Description:

This is a short animation I made in my Art 3 class to represent fear. There are many types of fears so I wanted to do a short story animation based on the fear of loud noises. This fear isnt usually someones first thought when asked "What are some types of fear?" so I chose one that is less known compared to a more common fear like claustrophobia.
