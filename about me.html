About The Artist

Hello, my name is Daysi Cuellar and I am a student at SJSU majoring in DMA. I have a focus on digital art but I like to venture out and try other mediums as well. Some of the main characteristics of my art can be described as mood, meaning, and subject. I mainly draw figures and have them either in a pose or location that is connected to meaning. When I venture out into backgrounds I mainly focus on the tone and mood with a smaller subject. My inspiration stems from my own experiences and love for stories. I am currently improving my art to begin creating my own webcomics and publishing them online. I also particularly want to try to create more landscape concept art. I hope to inspire other artists to create their own stories in the future!
