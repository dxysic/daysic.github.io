Project Description:

I drew this piece based on the storyline of one of my favorite games called Genshin Impact. This is based on the Inzamua lore during the Sokaku decree where the nation had been closed off to outside world. The god of the nation was the god of eternity but in search of eternity she overlooked what her people wanted and needed which led to a civil war. I wanted to represent how the god of the nation was all powerful but stayed within her realm instead of overseeing her nation.

Title: Inazuma's Shogun
